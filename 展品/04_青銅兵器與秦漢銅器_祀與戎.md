### 玉器-玉兵與玉具劍
![IMG_4618](img/IMG_4618.jpg)
「玉兵」與「玉具劍」 在西元前2000年以後，銅、鐵陸續成為製作帶刃器的主要質材。在階級森嚴的社會裡，飾有龍紋、雲紋的玉質工具、武器，主要用以象徵統治者高貴的身份。約自春秋時代開始，在劍與劍鞘的特定部位，飾以美玉。到漢代時發展成固定的四種玉飾：首、鐔、璏、摽（々ㄠㄟ）。四種玉飾齊備者，稱為「玉具劍」。十分貴重，可作為皇帝送給匈奴單于的國禮。 Jade Weaponry and Jade-Ornamented Swords After 2000 B.C.E., bronze and iron in turn became the main materials for sharp-blade weapons, while arms and tools made of jade with dragon or cloud patterns were privileges reserved for ruling classes only, signifying the owners' noble status in rigid hierarchical societies. Around the Spring and Autumn Period, beautiful jade started to adorn gentlemen's swords and eventually developed into four decorative fixtures during Han dynasty: shou (pommel), tan (cross-guard), wei (scabbard slide), piao (shape). Swords complete with all four ornaments, called yujujian (jade-ornamented sword), were highly valued and fit for state gifts from the Han emperors to the chieftains of Xiongnu.

# 望山

![IMG_4526](img/IMG_4526.jpg)

## 展品描述
望山 Regulator(wang-shan) 發射前手拉望山可使牙上升，以上弦裝箭。到了漢代，望山上常有刻度，可確認發射角度，相當於現代瞄準具標尺的功能。 Before lunching, pulling the wang-shan can raise the nut(ya) to hook the bowstring. # 郭

## 展品描述
郭 Shell(guo) 郭使弩機各零件更緊密連結，並避免直接接觸到弩臂，加固弩臂的機槽。最初的弩機並無郭，秦代開始出現帶郭的弩機，漢代起普遍使用有郭的弩機。 The guo prevents direct contact between the crossbow mechanism parts and the crossbow stock, reinforcing the groove of the crossbow stock. The early crossbow mechanisms did not have a guo. Crossbows with '郭' began to appear in the Qin Dynasty, and widely used from the Han Dynasty onwards.
